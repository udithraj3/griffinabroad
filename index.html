About Us

Griffin Abroad is a Hyderabad-based international education consultancy committed to guiding students toward academic success in the UK and Canada. With a strong presence in both India and Canada, our mission is to simplify the overseas education journey through personalized counselling, accurate university guidance, and end-to-end support—from shortlisting courses to securing admission offers.

At Griffin Abroad, we bridge ambition with opportunity. Whether you're seeking undergraduate, postgraduate, or diploma programs, we provide expert advice tailored to your academic background and future goals. We believe in building lasting relationships based on trust, transparency, and genuine care for your success abroad.

Our Services

  • University Admissions (UK & Canada): Personalized counselling to help you choose the right course, university, and location based on your profile and career goals.
  • Application & SOP Guidance: Professional help with filling applications, drafting compelling Statements of Purpose (SOPs), and preparing required documents.
  • Offer Letter Assistance: We work directly with universities and institutions to fast-track your offer letter process and keep you informed at every step.
  • Visa Guidance: Step-by-step support in compiling visa documents, financial proofs, and mock interview preparation.
  • Pre-Departure Support: Guidance on accommodation, travel, packing, and transitioning to life abroad.
  • Post-Arrival Assistance (Canada): Ongoing mentorship and support after landing—our team in Canada is here to help you settle in with ease.
